Young Artists of Distinction

Date Sun 07 May 2017

May 7th, 2017 2:00pm at Battelle Auditorium

Program:

Audrey Xu, age 10 – Piano Prelude & Fugue in C minor J.S. Bach
Michelle Fu, age 14 – Violin Sonata No. 5, Op. 24 Ludwig van Beethoven
Ourania Glezakou-Elbert, age 11 – Piano Polonaise in A-flat Frédéric Chopin
Noni Mosher, age 15 – Violin Serenade Dauber
Concerto in G minor, 1st mov. Bruch
Jordon Pratt, age 18 – Bassoon Lyric Suite Thomas Dunhill
Michelle Fu, age 14 – Piano Sonata No. 2, Scherzo Sergei Prokofiev
Winter Mallon, age 16 – Voice, Mezzo Soprano Liebhaber in Allen Gestalten Franz Schubert
Morning Has Broken Akiko and Forrest Kinney (arr.)
Taryn Boonpongmonee, age 11 – Piano L'Orage Friedrich Burgmüller
Marcos Rangel, age 17 – Alta Sax Pedro Interralde Pequeña Czarda
Allen Xiong, age 11 – Piano Humoreske Rodion Shchedrin
Libby Allen, age 18 – Bassoon Humoresque Reinhold Gliere
Sofia Marina, age 12 – Piano Diabolic Suggestions Sergei Prokofiev
Mariah Johnson, age 17 – Voice, Soprano Bel piacere G. F. Handel
When I Have Sung My Songs For You Ernest Charles
Anastasia Crosier, age 12 – Violin Csárdás Vittorio Mon

Teachers:

Lilia Moses, Frank Lu, Sherry Danielson, Adella Hammerstrom, Holly Harty, Cathy Exarhos, Hania Dec, and Cynthia Vaughn

Accompanists:

Peggy Patchett
Holly Harty
Benjamin Walley

Every year, Camerata celebrates the end of the season with a tribute to local music students. About 15-20 participants are selected from a group nominated by their teachers on the basis of performance at adjudications and competitions.

If you have questions about this event or would like to learn more, please send an email to youngartists@cameratamusica.com.
